Summary

Motovolt Urbn e-Bike is an electric two wheeler with a price tag ranging between Rs.49,999 to Rs. 54,999.It is available in 2 variants and 5 colours.Urbn e-Bike takes approx 4 Hours for a full charge and has a top speed of 25 kmph. ...


The price of Motovolt Urbn e-Bike in India starts at Rs. 49,999 and goes upto Rs. 54,999. Motovolt Urbn e-Bike comes with 2 variants which includes Urbn e-Bike STD. The top variant is Urbn e-Bike Smart Plus which comes at a price tag of Rs. 54,999. Motovolt smart cycles are India's best electric cycle and made it's name with best quality and riding experience.


Motovolt has launched the Urbn e-bike for a price of Rs 49,999 (ex-showroom). It gets a Lithium-ion removable battery offering a claimed range of upto 120 km and it can be fully charged in four hours.The Urbn e-bike can accelerate from 0-25kmph in less than 10 seconds and it also comes with pedal assist. Top speed is restricted to 25kmph as it’s a low-speed offering.
