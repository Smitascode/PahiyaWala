Summary

Goa-based startup Kabira Mobility has launched the KM 4000 electric bike at a price of Rs 1,36,990. This naked roadster is Kabira's flagship e-bike, powered by an 8kW motor that gives it a claimed top speed of 120kmph. Kabira claims a 0-40kmph acceleration time of 3.1 seconds. ...

The KM 4000's power source is a 4.4kWh battery pack which allows it a claimed range of 150km in Eco mode. Range in City mode is 110km while range in Sport mode is 90km.

Suspension duties are handled by a telescopic fork and monoshock, while braking power comes from twin discs up front, assisted by a single disc at the rear. CBS is standard. The KM 4000 will be sold through 18 dealers spread across Goa, north Karnataka, south Maharashtra, and Gujarat.
