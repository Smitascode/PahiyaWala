The Hero Lectro H5 is the latest electric cycle from the brand built on the GEMTEC platform which claims better ride geometry and smart ergonomics. ...

The Hero Lectro H3 is priced at Rs 29,999. It is powered by a 250W BLDC hub motor paired to a 5.8Ah Li-ion battery pack. It claims a 25kmph top speed and 30km of range on a single charge.
