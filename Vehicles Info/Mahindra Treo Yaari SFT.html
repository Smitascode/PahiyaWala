Exterior & Interior
Take a look at the Treo Yaari from any profile, it looks exactly like any other auto-rickshaw. It is hard to make out a battery-operated vehicle from the exterior. The overall design of the auto looks modern and contemporary, the wider windshield with a large wiper for safety, sizable tyres are capable of cruising through challenging terrain and the overall architecture of the auto-rickshaw makes Yaari rather appealing. The driver and passenger cabin looks spacious from the outside. The overall build quality, road presence, and body of the auto-rickshaw is solid giving it a worthy road presence.



Key Features
Treo Yaari scores high in terms of factory-fitted features that are not only useful and convenient but also more than any IC engine vehicle in its segment. The stand-out features include a windscreen & wiping system, spare wheel provision, different driving modes, a lockable glovebox, grab handles socket, hazard indicator, telematics unit & GPS, and 15 amp off-board charger (optional), etc. The Treo Yaari is part of the all-new platform that is designed and developed for Indian operating conditions. This makes the Yaari excellent in handling, driving, and daily trips. Treo Yaari’s driving quality is far better than its rivals with a spacious driver cabin and plenty of features. Treo Yaari gets global battery technology with zero maintenance, and lithium-ion batteries for more than five years of life. Mahindra is also offering cloud-based mobility platforms with remote monitoring of range, speed, location, and efficient fleet utilization. Treo Yaari is available in two variants Treo Yaari- SFT (D+4) with weather-resistant flex canopy and Treo Yaari - HRT (D+4) hardtop.

Performance
Since Treo Yaari is a 4-seater auto-rickshaw it needs more power and torque and also a higher powerful battery to last longer. Hence Mahindra is offering the Yaari with a top speed of 24.5 km/h, a certified 125 km of range, and the typical real-time driving range of 85 km. The gradeability is 7-degree. Powering the Treo Yaari is a 48V lithium-ion battery with a 3.69kWh of installed capacity. The battery charging time of 0-100% in standard conditions is 2 and half hours and produces a maximum power of 1.95kW, peak torque of 22 Nm tuned to a direct drive type transmission. Treo Yaari suspension at the front helical spring + damper + hydraulic shock absorber, the rigid rear axle, leaf spring & shock absorber, and brakes are mechanical types both at the front & rear. Dimensions include a wheelbase of 2073 mm, a turning radius of 2.9 meters, and a ground clearance of 142mm.
