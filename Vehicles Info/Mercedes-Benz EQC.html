Summary

The price of Mercedes-Benz EQC starts at Rs. 99.50 Lakh and goes upto Rs. 99.50 Lakh. Mercedes-Benz EQC is offered in 1 variants - the base model of EQC is 400 4MATIC and the top variant Mercedes-Benz EQC 400 4MATIC which comes at a price tag of Rs. 99.50 Lakh. ...


The EQC gets two asynchronous motors, one at each axle and the combined power output is 402 bhp. Mercedes claims a 0-100 kmph sprint time of just 5.1 seconds. The front motor is tuned for providing optimum, efficient performance while the rear motor is employed to offer a more dynamic feel when driving.
