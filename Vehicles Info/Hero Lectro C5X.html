Hero Lectro C5X is an electric two wheeler with a price tag of Rs.32,999.It is available in 1 variant and 1 colour.C5X takes approx 3 - 4 hours for a full charge and has a top speed of 25 kmph. ...


The price of Hero Lectro C5X starts at Rs. 32,999. Hero Lectro C5X is offered in 1 variant - C5X STD which comes at a price tag of Rs. 32,999.


Its new high-power lithium-ion removable battery can be fully charged in 3-4 hours. It’s also dust and waterproof (IP67 and IP65 rated) resistant. The cycle uses a 250W BLDC motor which when paried with the battery, is capable of offering a claimed range of up to 30km using peddle assist and 25km on throttle per charge. It can achieve a claimed top speed of 25kmph.
