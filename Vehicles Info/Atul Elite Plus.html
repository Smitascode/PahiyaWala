Summary

Atul Auto is a famous brand that makes a range of conventional three-wheelers in the passenger and cargo segment and now brings an equally capable portfolio of full-electric three-wheelers. The Elite Plus is a passenger carrier e-rickshaw for last-mile affordable mobility needs. ...

Atul Elite Plus Price: This full-electric passenger carrier auto-rickshaw starts from ₹ 1.12 lakh.
Atul Elite Plus GVW and Payload: This e-rickshaw can carry 3-4 passengers comfortable with a certified range of 80-100 km. The GVW is 699 kg and the Payload is 400 kg.

Atul Elite Plus Powertrain: The Elite Plus comes with an 850 / 1000 W power motor and the controller of 48V DC 40A. To make an affordable auto, it is fitted with the traditional lead-acid battery of 12V 100 AH.

Atul Elite Plus Range: Elite Plus offers 80 km of range per charge, which takes 8-10 hours of charging time. Still reasonably well for city or feeder routes applications. A customer can even change during lunchtime to get additional power. The maximum speed is 25 kmph, and Elite Plus can carry 4-passengers effortlessly, making it quite profitable. The Elite plus tyre size is 90/90-12, durable, light-weight for easy pickup, and longer range.

Atul Elite Plus Features: Some of the performance features include telescopic front suspension, 3 meters of turning circle diameter, the tyre can be interchanged among themselves. A halogen headlamp and steering lock for safety.

Atul Elite Plus Dimensions: Elite Plus is 699 kg gross vehicle weight e-auto. The overall dimensions of length, width, the height of 2800 mm, 1100 mm, and 1800mm. Atul Elite Plus is a promising e-auto to consider among the many local brands that cater to this segment. Atul Auto has a proven track record of offering high-quality products with all the necessary after-sales support. So, if you’re looking for an e-auto, visit the Atul Auto dealership to explore more about this vehicle.
